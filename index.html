Training and Development

A structured development or training program can maximize the skills and value of employees and policy leaders in an organization. J-Coleson Consulting develops and conducts training programs tailored to the specific needs of the municipality. Custom training sessions are provided to employees, supervisors and managers, and board/commission members. Some of our more recent and popular training workshops have related to performance management: Guidelines for Effective Performance Management; Communication; Making Decisions; Setting Goals and Objectives; Other areas for training have included: Customer Service; Strategic Planning; Basic Supervisory Skills for managers and supervisors. J-Coleson Consulting can assist in identifying training needs and develop an appropriate program and/or workshop to meet your specific requirements.

About us

J-Coleson Consulting is prepared to assist you with all your Human Resource Audit needs to ensure that your organization is in compliance with local, state, and federal laws, and to also ensure that the best practices in human resource management are being used. An HR Audit is a comprehensive and in-depth analysis of strengths and weaknesses and shows where improvements may be needed. Conducting an HR Audit involves a review of all your HR operations and systems, as well as practices, policies, and procedures, and may include benchmarking against organizations of similar size and/or industry.

J-Coleson Consulting regularly writes detailed personnel policies and personnel handbooks for SMEs. These policies provide management and employees with a ready reference and detailed information regarding all personnel and employment administration aspects. J-Coleson Consulting ensures that policies and procedures are written to reflect the most current local, state, and federal laws.
