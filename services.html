J-Coleson Consulting is prepared to assist you with all your Human Resource Audit needs to ensure that your organization is in compliance with local, state, and federal laws, and to also ensure that the best practices in human resource management are being used. An HR Audit is a comprehensive and in-depth analysis of strengths and weaknesses and shows where improvements may be needed. Conducting an HR Audit involves a review of all your HR operations and systems, as well as practices, policies, and procedures, and may include benchmarking against organizations of similar size and/or industry. J-Coleson Consulting can offer your company a comprehensive package of services to help ensure that your HR system, associated policies, practices and approach are contemporary and maintained in compliance with all local, state, and federal regulations. We also provide online services to assist you with maintaining critical HR documents.

J-Coleson Consulting regularly writes detailed personnel policies and personnel handbooks for SMEs. These policies provide management and employees with a ready reference and detailed information regarding all personnel and employment administration aspects. J-Coleson Consulting ensures that policies and procedures are written to reflect the most current local, state, and federal laws.
